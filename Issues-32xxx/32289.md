<div id="comment:7" align="right">comment:7</div>

If this code will be used elsewhere in the library, then of course its inclusion is fine (that was how the Gauss-Legendre routine made it in). Absent a strong use-case, however, perhaps it's at this point better to just let this ticket linger. There are numerical integration routines for tanh-sinh available in sage. There's [mpmath's integration](https://mpmath.org/doc/current/calculus/integration.html) and [PARI/GP](https://pari.math.u-bordeaux.fr/dochtml/html/Sums__products__integrals_and_similar_functions.html#se:intnum). Before we increase the maintenance burden of [SageMath](../wiki/SageMath) we probably want to have a motivation for why we do that.
