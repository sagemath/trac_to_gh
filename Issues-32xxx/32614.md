<a id='comment:14'></a>Still no hyphens allowed in `# optional - keyword`? In any case, please also change the documentation in `developer/coding_basics.rst` to match the behavior (currently says "Any punctuation (periods, commas, hyphens, semicolons, ...) after the first word ends the list of packages.").
