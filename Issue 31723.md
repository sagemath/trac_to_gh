# Issue 31723: Compute the Intersection Poset of a Cone of a Hyperplane Arrangement

Keywords: hyperplane arrangement, intersection poset, cone

A cone K of a hyperplane arrangement of A is an intersection of halfspaces defined by some of the hyperplanes of A (K is an open set). An intersection X of a cone K is an intersection of the arrangement A such that the intersection X has a nonempty intersection with K.
