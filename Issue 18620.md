`libfplll` is configured with

```
./configure --prefix="$SAGE_LOCAL" --libdir="$SAGE_LOCAL/lib" --includedir="$SAGE_LOCAL/include/fplll"
```

leading to the double subdirectory `local/include/fplll/fplll`.
