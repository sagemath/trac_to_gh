Extends the current functionaly for hypersurfaces to the complete intersection of two quadratics in `P^3`

```
    sage: R.<w,x,y,z> = QQ[]
    sage: quadratic1 = w^2+x^2+y^2
    sage: quadratic2 = z^2 + w*x
    sage: WeierstrassForm([quadratic1, quadratic2])
    (-1/4, 0)
```
