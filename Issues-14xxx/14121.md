Shuffle product contains method does not work properly.

```
sage: w = Word('ab')
sage: x = Word('ac')
sage: w.shuffle(x)
Shuffle product of word: ab and word: ac
sage: s = w.shuffle(x)
sage: s.list()
[word: abac, word: aabc, word: aacb, word: aabc, word: aacb, word: acab]
sage: x*w in s
False
```
