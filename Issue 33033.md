A sign error in one of the examples in [Contour Plots](https://doc.sagemath.org/html/en/reference/plotting/sage/plot/contour_plot.html) leads to an unexpected image.

```
sage: f(x, y)=x^2-y^2
 BAD             ^
```
![](ticket:33270:tmp_ynxwu9m7.png)

  OK             ^
