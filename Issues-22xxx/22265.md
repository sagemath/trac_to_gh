```

A.<z>=AffineSpace(R,1)
H=End(A)
f=H([z^2+c])
f.dynatomic_polynomial([1,2])
```

R.<c>=QQ[]
P.<x,y>=ProjectiveSpace(R,1)
H=End(P)
f=H([x^2+c*y^2,y^2])
f.dynatomic_polynomial([1,2]).parent()
