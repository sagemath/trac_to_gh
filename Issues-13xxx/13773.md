Another example at #24800:

```
var('r2 si co r12 r22 r32 d32')
eq1 = r12==r2*d32*(1-si*(co+sqrt(3*(1-co*co)))/2)/2
eq2 = r22==r2*d32*(1-si*(co-sqrt(3*(1-co*co)))/2)/2
eq3 = r32==r2*d32*(1+si*co)/2
solve([eq1,eq2,eq3],r2,si,co)
```
