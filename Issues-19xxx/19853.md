The known series for `asec`/`acsc` are not implemented:

```
sage: asec(x).series(x==oo)
(arcsec(+Infinity)) + Order(+Infinity)

(arccsc(+Infinity)) + Order(+Infinity)
```

* http://mathworld.wolfram.com/InverseSecant.html
