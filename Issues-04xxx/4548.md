```
There's a problem with powers of negative numbers the latex method for
symbolic arithmetic.
In version 3.1.4 I get this:
{{{
sage: var('n')
n
sage: latex((-1)^n)
{-1}^{n}

It should, of course, be {(-1)}^{n}.
 -- Anders
