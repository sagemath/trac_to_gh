Basically, we are making all external libraries optional, except for a minimal set of "core" libraries such as `cysignals` and `gmpy2` (see #29865). 

(from #30371): See #29705 under the items titled "Deploy mildly modularized distributions", "Further modularization" for a sketch of such distributions.
