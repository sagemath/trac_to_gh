The method `factor` doesn't correctly factor symbolic expressions over the complex numbers.

```
sage: a, b = var("a, b")
sage: (a^2 - 2*I*a*b - b^2).factor()
a^2 - 2*I*a*b - b^2
sage: ((a - I*b)^2).expand()
a^2 - 2*I*a*b - b^2
```
