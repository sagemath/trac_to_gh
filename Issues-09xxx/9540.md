  From: Number Field in I with defining polynomial x^2 + 1
  To:   Integer Ring

<a id='comment:4'></a>The conversion from `QQ` to `ZZ` actually bypasses the `_integer_` method: instead, it uses `sage.rational.Q_to_Z` (discovered as the canonical section of the coercion in the reverse direction). No such fast conversion is implemented for quadratic number field elements.
