<a id='comment:2'></a>This issue is with `__cmp__` and the assumption that there is a total ordering on Weyl groups. I think the `__cmp__` was needed for old Sage (python?) things, and the usual line of `==` is not the same as being isomorphic. IMO the examples above should not be equal. So I believe we should change the behavior of `__cmp__` to fallback to comparison of the strings, or better yet, remove it altogether.
