sage: (QQ^2).submodule([[1,1]])
Vector space of degree 2 and dimension 1 over Rational Field
Basis matrix:
[1 1]
sage: _.category()
Category of finite dimensional vector spaces with basis over (number fields and quotient fields and metric spaces)
