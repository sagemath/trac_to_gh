The examples could indeed include a `figsize` setting.

So we could change the examples along the lines of

```diff
- sage: complex_plot(lambda z: z, (-3, 3), (-3, 3))
+ sage: G = complex_plot(lambda z: z, (-3, 3), (-3, 3))
+ sage: G.show(figsize=8)
```
