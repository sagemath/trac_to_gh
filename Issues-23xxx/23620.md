Currently, the parent of a gcd might be wrong when the gcd of polynomials is just the gcd of its contents:

```
sage: R.<x> = ZpFM(2)[]
sage: f = 2*x + 2
sage: g = 4*x + 2
sage: f.gcd(g).parent() is R
False # parent is ZpFM(2)
```
