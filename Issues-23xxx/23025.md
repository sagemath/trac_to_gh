Given a polynomial f, finding (if any) g and h such that f = g o h is called
"decomposition of polynomials".

See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polynomial_decomposition.

This does not seem to be available in Sage.

Maxima : https://maxima.sourceforge.io/docs/manual/maxima_76.html#polydecomp
