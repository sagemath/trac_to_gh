I would also instead do this change:

```diff
diff --git a/src/sage/interfaces/kash.py b/src/sage/interfaces/kash.py
index a0d8ad5..a9a9515 100644
--- a/src/sage/interfaces/kash.py

@@ -517,10 +517,11 @@ class Kash(Expect):
```
