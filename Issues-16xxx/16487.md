Currently it only prints in French convention for non `repr` formats:

```
sage: t = Tableau([[1,2,3],[4,5]])
sage: Tableaux.global_options(ascii_art="compact")
|4|5|
|1|2|3|
sage: Tableaux.global_options(ascii_art="table")
sage: ascii_art(t)
+---+---+
| 4 | 5 |
+---+---+---+
| 1 | 2 | 3 |
+---+---+---+
```
