```
sage: var('t')
t
sage: F = NumberField(t^2+1, 'a')
sage: R.<x,y> = F[]
sage: type(x.coefficients()[0])
<type 'sage.rings.number_field.number_field_element.NumberFieldElement_absolute'>
sage: F(1) + x.coefficients()[0]
```
