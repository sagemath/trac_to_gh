```
sage: sage: s = SymmetricFunctions(QQ).schur()
sage: sage: s.sum_of_terms([ (Partition([i]), i) for i in range(3) ])
s[1] + 2*s[2]
sage: sage: s.sum_of_terms([ (Partition([i]), i) for i in range(3) ]) * 1
