I just noticed in Wolfram's explanation of algorithms that they do not overlay their mesh on their figure (like in this ticket).  Instead, it appears that they build their triangles based on their mesh: "Mesh lines and contour lines are an integral part of the geometry, not overlays. (see http://reference.wolfram.com/mathematica/note/SomeNotesOnInternalImplementation.html)
