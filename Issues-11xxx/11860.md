Returns the degree of a homogeneous ideal.

EXAMPLES::

```
sage: R.<x,y> = QQ[]
sage: I = R.ideal(x+y)
sage: I.degree()
1
sage: J = R.ideal(x^2+y)
sage: J.degree()

...
```
