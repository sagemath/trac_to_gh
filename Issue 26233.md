Since 8.3, `CBF("1+I")` and `CBF("1+i")` stopped working.

However,  any string representing a purely real float, i.e. without an "I" or "i",  works fine, e.g.: `CBF("3.14")` gives `[3.134212321413100 +/- 5.34e-16]`
