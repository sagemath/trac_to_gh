```
sage: p = MixedIntegerLinearProgram(solver="CVXOPT")

...

<repr(<sage.numerical.mip.MixedIntegerLinearProgram at 0x190970a70>) failed: TypeError: cannot concatenate 'str' and 'NoneType' objects>
```
