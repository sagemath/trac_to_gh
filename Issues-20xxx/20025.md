Playing with finite fields on Sage 6.10 or 7.0:

```
GF(30011**(19*3), name='z')
...
ValueError: the order of a finite field must be a prime power
```

http://pari.math.u-bordeaux.fr/cgi-bin/bugreport.cgi?bug=1782
