<a id='comment:9'></a>I did some further cleaning up on a new branch `u/pbruin/15855-weierstrass_p_cleanup`.  The test for `p >= prec + 3` can also be done at the beginning, since *p* really arise in the denominators of the series, we are not just dividing by it as an intermediate step of the algorithm.
