```
sage: x = 1 + 5 + O(5^6)
sage: y = 1 + 5 + O(5^5)
sage: x.log()
 5 + 2*5^2 + 4*5^3 + 2*5^4 + O(5^6)
sage: y.log()
 5 + 2*5^2 + 4*5^3 + 5^4 + O(5^5)
```

The answers should agree mod 5!^5. I bet someone forgot to take into account the p-adic valuation of the denominators in the series.
