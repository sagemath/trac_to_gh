We see here that _interface_init_() on RDF loses the last few digits of its value, by truncation.

```
sage: RR(RDF(sin(1)))
0.841470984807897
sage: RR(RDF(sin(1))._interface_init_())
0.841470984808000
```

I should have a patch for this very soon.
