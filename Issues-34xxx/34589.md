I have two questions regarding the inheritance structure:

1/ There is a disymmetry in the MRO between `FiniteRankFreeModule`, `FiniteDualFreeModule` and `TensorFreeModule`: for the first two classes, the inheritance order is `(ReflexiveModule_(base or dual), FiniteRankFreeModule_abstract)`,  while for the third class it is the opposite order: `(FiniteRankFreeModule_abstract, ReflexiveModule_tensor)`.
